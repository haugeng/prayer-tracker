User Guide

A lightweight tracker for prayer sessions and digital journaling.

🔒 Your Privacy

None of your data from this app—your notes or your timings—ever leaves your phone or computer. There is no central database and no one (including the developer) can see what you write. It stays 100% on your device, just like a paper journal.

📲 Data

As all data is only stored on your device, use JSON Backup regularly. If you get a new phone or switch browsers, you'll need this file to transfer your history.
