User Guide

⏺ Recording Sessions

Use the Timer tab to record in real-time. Session stays active once the timer starts. Type your prayer pointers or reflections in the textbox.

✍️ Manual Entry

Use the Log tab to save past sessions. Select your start and end times, and type in your prayer pointers or reflections.

📅 Managing History

Tap any day in History to view the sessions of that day. Tap the daily goal, time or duration to edit them. Type directly in the text area to edit the notes.

💾 Data & Privacy

All data stays on your device only. Nobody can read your notes. Use JSON Backup regularly. This can be used to restore your history to another device or browser.
