User Guide

A lightweight, private, and distraction-free web app designed to help you track your prayer sessions and keep a digital journal of your prayer pointers.

üìñ Manual Logging

Missed a session? Use the Log tab to backdate entries. Simply select your start and end times, paste your pointers, and save.

üì≤ Transferring Data

To move your data to a new phone or browser:

  1. On the old device, go to History and tap Download JSON Backup.
  2. Send that file to your new device.
  3. On the new device, open the app, go to History, tap Restore from Backup, and select the file.

üîí Privacy

Your data is stored locally in your browser's memory. It never touches a server. Please backup regularly to avoid data loss if you clear your browser cache.
